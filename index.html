About Us


Meet the Duo



We are Hui Lin and Fede, an acoustic duo based in Frankfurt am Main. Armed with a guitar and vocals, we take you from the 60s to 2000s across the genres of pop, rock and everything in between, delivering unique interpretations of songs we love.

From cosy café gigs to private events, we love connecting with audiences through music. Check out our videos and get in touch!
